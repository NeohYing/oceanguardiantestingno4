Our Mission

Many Marine Species
Face Extinction

Plastic pollution, overfishing, habitat destruction, and climate change are threatening marine life around the world. As a result, many species face declining populations and an uncertain future.

Our objective is to:

Raise awareness about endangered marine species

Encourage action that doesn't harm marine life

Promote ocean conservation through everyday actions
