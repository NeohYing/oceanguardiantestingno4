The deeper you go into the ocean, the less we know! Midnight Zone, The Abyss, and Trenches are among the least explored places on Earth, so many deep-sea animals have not even been fully studied yet.

The deepest ocean zones contain fewer known endangered species—not necessarily because they're safer, but because many animals living there have not been studied enough to assess their conservation status.
