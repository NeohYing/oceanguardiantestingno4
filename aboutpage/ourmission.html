Ocean Guardian is driven by a clear mission and vision to inspire meaningful action for marine conservation.

Our Mission

To educate and inspire people to protect endangered marine animals and marine ecosystems by increasing awareness of ocean threats and encouraging sustainable everyday actions.

  • Educate communities
  • Raise awareness
  • Encourage action

Our Vision

A future where healthy oceans and thriving marine life are protected by informed communities who work together to conserve our blue planet.

  • Healthy oceans
  • Protected wildlife
  • Sustainable future
