Threats to Our Oceans

Plastic Pollution

Plastic waste harms marine life through ingestion and entanglement.

Overfishing

Excessive fishing disrupts marine food chains and reduces biodiversity.

Climate Change

Rising ocean temperatures and coral bleaching threaten marine ecosystems worldwide.
